CNC Machine Motherboard

Designed my own CNC motherboard capable of milling metal by using a TMC5160 stepper motor driver, a STM32 MCU, and low charge MOSFETS that can withstand high voltage fluctuations while simultaneously running at 48V. Additional capabilities include breakout pins for cooling, limit switches, E-stop and micro-sd expansion for saving G-code. Major challenges included signal isolation and surge protection.
